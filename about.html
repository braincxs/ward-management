Our Impact

SDG 3: Good Health & Well-being

We are committed to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 3. Our technology is built to hit specific global targets to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages.

Target 3.c (Health Workforce Support)

  • Cut the Busy Work: We automate bed tracking and note-taking so staff can spend their time with patients, not paperwork.
  • Fight Burnout: By removing tedious admin tasks, we lower stress and help prevent staff exhaustion.
  • Work on the Move: Mobile tools let doctors and nurses update info instantly, making their shifts smoother and faster.
  • Better Focus: When the system handles the data, the team can focus entirely on saving lives.

About Team Error 404

We are a group of passionate developers and designers dedicated to solving "unsolvable" problems in healthcare. We chose our name because we believe that in a hospital, a "404 Error" (data not found) can be the difference between life and death.

Our team specializes in creating high-performance, user-centric web applications that handle sensitive data with speed and security. We combine modern tech with human-centric design to build tools that medical professionals actually enjoy using.

Our Mission

"To eliminate information gaps in healthcare by providing every medical professional with the data they need, the moment they need it."

Our mission is to ensure that no patient is ever a "missing record." We strive to empower hospitals with digital tools that are as reliable as the doctors and nurses who use them.
